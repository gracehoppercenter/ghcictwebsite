Worker Cooperatives

Worker cooperatives are businesses owned and democratically controlled by their employees. Unlike traditional companies where decisions flow from the top down, worker co-ops operate on the principle of "one worker, one vote."

Solidarity Economy

The solidarity economy represents a global movement to create an economic system based on cooperation, sustainability, and meeting human needs‚Äînot just maximizing profit.
